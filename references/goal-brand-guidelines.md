# GOAL Brand Guidelines

## Brand Colors

**Primary Brand Color:** `#077BE5` (GOAL Blue)

**Supporting Colors:**
- Dark Blue: `#00172D`
- Accent Color 1: `#3AEECA` (Teal)
- Accent Color 2: `#97C2E8` (Light Blue)
- Accent Color 3: `#9FDACD` (Mint)
- Light Grey: `#F8F9FC`
- Text: `#001020`

## Logo Usage

**Logo File:** `goal-logo-light.png` (located in `templates/`)

**Placement Guidelines:**
- **Presentations:** Top-right corner of content slides (140px width), top-left or top-right on title/closing slides (180-200px width)
- **Documents:** Top-left corner of first page or header area

**Absolute Path for HTML:** Always use absolute paths when referencing the logo in HTML files:
```html
<img src="/path/to/goal-logo-light.png" alt="GOAL Logo">
```

## Typography

**Primary Font:** Inter (Google Fonts)
- Weights: 400 (Regular), 600 (Semi-Bold), 700 (Bold), 900 (Black)

**Font Hierarchy:**
- **Presentation Titles:** 84-96px, weight 900
- **Slide Headers:** 48px, weight 900
- **Body Text:** 18-24px, weight 400-500
- **Labels/Captions:** 14-18px, weight 600-700

## Design Principles

**Style:** Swiss International Style with clean grid systems, bold typography, and minimal decoration

**Visual Elements:**
- Use geometric accents (lines, circles) sparingly
- Maintain ample white space
- Avoid rounded corners, card components, or shadowed boxes
- Use solid colors and clean borders

**Color Application:**
- Use GOAL Blue (`#077BE5`) for primary accents, headers, highlights, and key data points
- Apply colors consistently across all materials
- Ensure sufficient contrast for readability
- Limit to 2-3 colors per slide/page for consistency

## Content Standards

**Tone:** Professional, authoritative, and data-driven

**Format:** Use imperative, action-oriented language for instructions and SOPs

**Quality:** All content must be factual, verifiable, and based on actual training materials—never fabricate information
